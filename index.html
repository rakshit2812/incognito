With the dream to shape and bring out the latent talents of the students, the Cultural Society, INCOGNITO, of Indian Institute of Information and Technology — Kota, was established in 2018.

At Incognito, we aim to bring in out the best of our skills, we teach, we learn, we perform, and, we win, we collaborate and show to the world what we the students at IIIT Kota are made up of. Incognito openly conducts and promotes, through fests, participation in all forms of : Dance, Dramatics, Music / Singing.
